**Finance:**
- Invoice 1st of month, due 5th, follow up 6th, suspend 15th
- All payments and costs through business account only
- No full-time hires before 5 paying clients

## Content Strategy Principles (UAE Market)

**What works:**
- 30–45 sec Reels with hard cut at 0.5 seconds
- Arabic text overlays on English videos (doubles reach)
- Result-proof content (before/after, stats, screenshots) = 3x more saves
- Premium Dubai location tags (Marina, DIFC, Downtown)
- Agency accountability content = blue ocean in UAE — nobody shows real metrics
- Best posting time: Thursday 6–9 PM UAE

**Avoid:**
- Generic "5 tips" carousels with no visual interest
- Logo intro or music fade-in at the start of Reels
- "Follow us for more" as the only CTA
- Real estate / Lamborghini flex content — UAE audience is numb to it
