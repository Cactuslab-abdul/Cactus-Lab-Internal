## UAE Market Research — Key Findings

### What's Working (May 2026)
1. **30–45 second Reels with hard cut at 0.5 seconds** — UAE scrolls faster than global average
2. **Arabic text overlays on English videos** — doubles reach into Arabic-speaking audience
3. **Premium Dubai location tags** — Dubai Marina, DIFC, Downtown: 40–60% more reach
4. **Thursday 6–9 PM UAE** — highest-reach posting window across all niches
5. **Result-proof content** — before/after, stats, screenshots: 3× more saves
6. **Agency accountability content** — TOTAL BLUE OCEAN in UAE. Nobody shows real metrics.

### Oversaturated — Avoid
- Generic "5 tips" carousels with no visual interest
- Logo intro / music fade-in at the start of Reels
- "Follow us for more" as the only CTA
- Real estate / Lamborghini flex content — audience is numb

### Top Performing Hooks (UAE)
- `"Dubai businesses are losing money because of this one mistake…"` — loss aversion
- `"We grew [brand] from 0 to [result] in [time] — here's exactly what we did"` — proof hook
- `"Nobody in Dubai is doing this — and it's working"` — exclusivity / FOMO
- `"The real reason your Instagram isn't growing (it's not what you think)"` — pattern interrupt
- `"POV: You've been in Dubai 3 years and still don't know about this"` — expat identity
