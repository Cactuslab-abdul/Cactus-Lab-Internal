# Admin & Finance

## Pricing Structure

| | |
|---|---|
| **Monthly retainer** | AED 5,500 (anchor — never open below this) |
| **Minimum acceptable** | AED 4,500 (only as a closing concession) |
| **Revenue target** | AED 5,500 × number of active clients |
| **Operating cost target** | Under AED 3,000/month until 5 clients |

---

## Invoicing

- **Invoice date:** 1st of each month
- **Payment due:** 5th of each month
- **Late payment follow-up:** Day 6
- **Service suspension:** Day 15 (withheld deliverables)
- **Tool:** Wave (free) or Zoho Invoice — professional, trackable

**For new clients:**
- 50% upfront before work starts
- 50% on completion / per milestones
- No work begins without signed contract AND first payment received

---

## Contracts

**Template:** `~/AwabHQ/Cactus Lab/Operations/Service Agreement (Cactus Lab).docx`

**Required clauses in every contract:**
- Scope of work (15 videos/month minimum)
- 1 revision round per video
- 30-day cancellation notice
- Payment terms (50/50 or monthly advance)
- Late payment clause (pause day 6, charge day 15)
- Out-of-scope billing

**Process:**
1. Send as PDF via WhatsApp
2. Request signed copy back before work begins
3. Store signed contract in Drive: `/Admin/Contracts/[Client Name]`

**Never start work without a signed contract and first invoice paid.**
