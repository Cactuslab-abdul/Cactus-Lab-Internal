# Team & Hiring

## Current Setup (0–3 Clients)

**Editor arrangement:**
- Paid per video delivered — not a monthly salary
- Keeps costs variable as you scale
- SLA written into agreement: 48hr turnaround, 1 revision round included, additional revisions charged

**Founder rule:** No full-time hires until 5 paying clients — keep cost base lean.

**Shoot workflow (document and standardise):**
- Gear checklist
- Shot list template
- Minimum shots required per video type

---

## Scaling Triggers

| Client count | Hire |
|-------------|------|
| 4 clients | Part-time content coordinator — scheduling, captions, story reposts (AED 2,000–3,000/month) |
| 6 clients | Second shooter — per-project revenue share (not salary) |
| 8 clients | Operations coordinator — client communication and reporting |
| 10 clients | Review full team structure — this is agency stage, not freelancer stage |

---

## Hiring Principles

1. **Never hire someone to fix a problem** — hire someone to scale a solution that already works
2. **Trial first** — paid test project before committing to a retainer
3. **Keep all non-founder roles variable cost** until revenue is consistent for 3+ months

## Own Marketing (Cactus Lab's Instagram)

- Post 3x/week minimum — always short-form video, never static-only
- **Content pillars:** BTS of shoots · Client results (with permission) · Content tips · Niche-specific hooks
- **Never post:** "we offer social media management" — show the work, show results, show the process
- Use your own IG as a live case study: document growth from 0 → 1k → 10k
- Target 1,000 followers before leading with growth as a pitch point

**Weekly outbound:**
- One niche-targeted DM campaign per week (same niche, 30 businesses in one day)
- One "results post" per closed client
- One "behind the shoot" reel per week
- Collect one video testimonial from Pets Delight this month and post it
