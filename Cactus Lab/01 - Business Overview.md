# Business Overview

## What Cactus Lab Does

Cactus Lab is a short-form video content and social media management agency serving UAE businesses. The core value proposition: **clients never need to appear on camera or manage posting themselves.** Cactus Lab handles scripting, filming, editing, scheduling, and reporting end-to-end.

**Deliverables per client per month:**
- 15 short-form videos (Reels, TikToks, Shorts)
- Full social media scheduling (Later or Buffer)
- Daily story reposts (max 15 min/client/day)
- Monthly performance report (sent by day 3 of following month)
- Bi-weekly performance update (views, followers gained, top post)

---

## Positioning

**One-liner:** "Content that actually works — without you lifting a finger."

**Key differentiator:** No client on set needed. Cactus Lab scripts, films, edits, and posts everything. The client's only job is a 20-min planning call at the start of each month and approving content within 24 hours.

**Target niches (UAE):**
- Perfume & watches (#dubaiperfume, #dubaiwatch)
- Car businesses (#dubaigarage)
- Recruitment agencies (#dubairecruiter)
- Spice/food businesses (#dubaispices)
- Pet businesses (proven — Pets Delight is current client)

**Prospecting geography:** Dubai (Al Quoz Mon, Deira Tue) + Sharjah Industrial (Thu)

## Service Agreement (Key Terms)

- **Payment:** 50% upfront before work begins, 50% on completion (or milestones)
- **Monthly retainers:** Paid in advance — no work starts without receipt of payment
- **Revisions:** 1 round per video — written into every contract
- **Approvals:** Deliverables accepted if client doesn't respond within 3 business days
- **Timelines:** Client delays auto-extend deadlines
- **Late payment:** Services suspended day 6, deliverables withheld day 15
- **Cancellation:** 30-day notice clause
- **Out-of-scope:** Any work beyond the agreed SOW billed separately
